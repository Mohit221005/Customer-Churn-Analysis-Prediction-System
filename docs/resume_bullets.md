# Resume-Ready Project Bullets

## Strong Resume Version

- Built an end-to-end customer churn analysis and prediction system using Python, SQL, and Power BI guidance to identify at-risk customers and support retention decisions.
- Developed a reusable preprocessing pipeline in pandas and scikit-learn, including rule-based `TotalCharges` cleaning, target normalization, and business-focused feature engineering such as `TenureGroup` and `NumAddOnServices`.
- Performed churn-focused EDA to quantify risk patterns across contract type, tenure, pricing, payment behavior, and support-service adoption, then exported BI-ready charts and summary tables.
- Wrote production-style SQL queries to generate churn KPIs, segment-level churn analysis, high-risk customer extraction, and dashboard-ready matrices for business reporting.
- Trained and compared Logistic Regression and Random Forest churn models using stratified train-test splitting and evaluated performance with accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score.
- Converted model outputs into business actions by designing risk tiers, retention playbooks, high-risk customer extracts, and projected retained-revenue estimates for targeted save campaigns.
- Designed a 3-page Power BI dashboard blueprint with executive KPIs, churn-driver analysis, operational targeting views, and reusable DAX measures.
